Oncology

The Hematology & Oncology Clinic provides integrated services for the diagnosis and treatment of both oncology and hematology cases.

Our clinic offers comprehensive care supported by advanced medical procedures and professional expertise

Types of Procedures
  1. Tumor Surgery
    A surgical procedure to remove tumors, aiming to prevent cancer spread, inhibit malignancy growth, and assist in diagnostic evaluation.
  2. Systemic Cancer Therapy:
    • Cytostatics (Chemotherapy): Drugs designed to destroy or inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
    • Targeted Therapy: Specifically targets cancer cells with minimal impact on healthy tissue.
    • Immunotherapy: Enhances the immune system or helps it identify and destroy cancer cells.
    • Hormonal Therapy: Blocks the hormones that cancer cells need to grow and develop.
  3. HSCT (Hematopoietic Stem Cell Transplantation)
    A medical procedure used to restore bone marrow function, especially for patients with blood cancers such as leukemia.
  4. Robotic Prostate Biopsy
    A minimally invasive procedure using robotic technology to obtain prostate tissue samples with high precision for accurate diagnosis.
  5. Supportive and Palliative Care Therapy
    Care designed to improve quality of life for patients and their families while facing serious or chronic illness.
