Advanced Infection Control

We implement Infection Control Protocols (PPI) according to the highest Ministry of Health standards to create a safe and hygienic hospital environment for patients, families, and medical staff.

State-of-the-Art Laboratory Services

The Medistra Laboratory focuses on the quality and accuracy of test results. This commitment is proven by the acquisition of international quality standard accreditation ISO 15189:2022 and national KARS accreditation, ensuring all services meet the highest standards in quality and patient safety.

Supported by advanced automated and biomolecular technology, we ensure that every testing process is fast and precise. The integrated digital system, Laboratory Information System (LIS) and Hospital Information System (HIS), guarantees accurate, secure, and efficient results transfer.

All operations are run by a team of specialist doctors and trained analysts with Biosafety Level 1 and 2 (BSL-1, BSL-2) standards that guarantee safety. To maintain consistency, we implement a strict Total Quality Assurance system at both national and international levels.
