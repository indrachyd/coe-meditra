Medistra's Tropical and Infectious Disease Clinic provides comprehensive diagnostic and treatment services for a wide range of infectious and tropical diseases.

Layanan Penyakit Tropis dan Infeksius Medistra Hospital menawarkan layanan diagnosis dan perawatan komprehensif untuk berbagai infeksi menular dan penyakit tropis.

Our team handles cases such as typhoid, dengue, malaria, chikungunya, leptospirosis, filariasis, and helminthiasis, as well as complex acute and chronic infections.

Kami menangani kasus penyakit seperti typhoid, dengue, malaria, chikungunya, leptospirosis, filariasis, dan helmintiasis, serta berbagai infeksi lain yang bersifat kompleks, baik akut maupun kronis.

We are also highly experienced in managing sepsis and septic shock, along with infections affecting various organs including the bones and joints, brain, respiratory tract, heart, abdomen, kidneys and urinary tract, and those related to organ transplantation.

Tim di Medistra Hospital juga memiliki keahlian dalam menangani sepsis dan syok sepsis, serta infeksi pada berbagai organ tubuh, termasuk tulang dan sendi, otak, paru-paru, jantung, abdomen, ginjal dan saluran kemih, hingga infeksi terkait transplantasi organ.

Advanced Infectious Disease Care Layanan Unggulan Penyakit Tropis dan Infeksius

We offer care for patients with HIV/AIDS, tuberculosis (TB), and infections caused by bacteria, mycoplasma, viruses, fungi, and parasites.

Kami melayani pasien dengan penyakit HIV/AIDS, tuberkulosis (TBC), serta infeksi akibat bakteri, mycoplasma, virus, jamur, dan parasit.

Viral infections treated include RSV, influenza, Covid-19, meningitis, hepatitis, polio, herpes, varicella, and more.

Untuk infeksi virus, kami menangani kasus seperti RSV, influenza, Covid-19, meningitis, hepatitis, polio, herpes, varicella, dan lainnya.

Fungal infections such as pneumocystis pneumonia, cryptococcosis, candidiasis, aspergillosis, and histoplasmosis are also within our expertise.

Sementara untuk infeksi jamur, kami mengelola kasus pneumocystis pneumonia, kriptokokus, kandidiasis, aspergillosis, histoplasmosis, dan infeksi jamur lainnya.

We also manage parasitic infections including malaria, toxoplasmosis, and amebiasis as part of our integrated care approach.

Penanganan infeksi parasit seperti malaria, toksoplasmosis, dan amubiasis juga menjadi bagian dari cakupan layanan kami.

Diagnostic Capabilities Pemeriksaan Diagnostik Terkini

Our clinic is equipped with modern diagnostic technologies to ensure fast and accurate results. The Arbovirus PCR Test allows for early detection of viruses such as chikungunya, dengue, Zika, West Nile, and yellow fever.

Klinik kami didukung oleh teknologi diagnostik modern untuk mendapatkan diagnosis yang cepat dan akurat. Pemeriksaan laboratorium canggih seperti PCR Arbovirus dapat mendeteksi virus seperti chikungunya, dengue, Zika, West Nile, dan yellow fever.

In addition, we offer PCR Multiplex Biofire, a rapid molecular testing system capable of identifying up to 22 different pathogens associated with conditions like meningitis/encephalitis, gastrointestinal infections, pneumonia, and respiratory diseases, all within approximately 45 minutes.

Selain itu, kami juga menyediakan PCR Multiplex Biofire, yaitu sistem tes molekuler yang mampu mendeteksi hingga 22 patogen berbeda untuk kasus seperti meningitis/ensefalitis, infeksi gastrointestinal, pneumonia, dan gangguan pernapasan. Prosedur ini menghasilkan hasil dalam waktu sekitar 45 menit.

We also provide GeneXpert Mtb and Urine LAM Antigen testing for TB, and Procalcitonin testing for detecting severe bacterial infections.

Pemeriksaan lainnya seperti Gen Expert Mtb dan Antigen LAM urin untuk TBC, serta Procalcitonin untuk deteksi infeksi bakteri berat juga tersedia.
