Range of Service

Endoscopic Procedures

We offer a full spectrum of diagnostic and therapeutic endoscopic procedures, including:

  • Gastroscopy
  • Colonoscopy
  • Rectoscopy
  • Capsule Endoscopy
  • Enteroscopy

ERCP (Endoscopic Retrograde Cholangiopancreatography)

A specialized procedure that combines endoscopy and X-ray imaging to diagnose and treat conditions in the bile ducts and pancreatic ducts.

Advanced Diagnostic and Therapeutic Endoscopy

Our services include:

  • Placement of enteral and biliary stents
  • Management of esophageal varices
  • Small bowel enteroscopy
  • Endoscopic treatments for gastrointestinal bleeding
  • Endoscopic ultrasound (EUS)

Endoscopic Sleeve Gastroplasty

A minimally invasive weight-loss procedure. Unlike conventional surgery, this technique uses an endoscope to suture the stomach, eliminating the need for incisions and minimizing the risk of complications. It enables patients to return to normal activities more quickly.

Intragastric Balloon

A non-surgical weight-loss method that involves placing a special balloon inside the stomach to help reduce appetite.

Bariatric Surgery

A surgical intervention designed to support significant weight loss by modifying the digestive system.

Hepatobiliary Disease Diagnosis and Treatment

Includes procedures such as liver biopsy and pancreatic biopsy for diagnosing and managing liver and pancreatic conditions.

PEG (Percutaneous Endoscopic Gastrostomy)

A procedure that places a feeding tube directly into the stomach through the skin for patients who require long-term nutritional support.

Non-Endoscopic Treatments

We also provide care for conditions such as hemorrhoids using non-endoscopic techniques.
