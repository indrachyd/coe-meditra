Comprehensive Orthopedic Services at Medistra Hospital

Medistra Hospital offers comprehensive orthopedic services to address a wide range of conditions affecting bones, muscles, and soft tissues.

Their team of experienced and highly trained orthopedic specialists manages everything from sports injuries and fractures to degenerative diseases such as osteoarthritis.

Orthopedic Services
  1. Total Knee Robotic Surgery
    A total knee replacement (TKR) procedure performed with robotic assistance to enhance surgical precision and optimize recovery outcomes.
  2. Total Hip Replacement Surgery
    Surgical replacement of the hip joint with an artificial implant to reduce chronic pain and restore mobility.
  3. Spine Surgery
    Surgical procedures aimed at addressing various structural or neurological issues in the spine, including nerve compression and spinal deformities.
  4. Spine Endoscopy Surgery
    A minimally invasive procedure using endoscopic technology to treat spinal disorders with smaller incisions and faster recovery time.
  5. Arthroscopy
    A minimally invasive surgery to treat joint injuries such as ACL, PCL, and meniscus tears.
  6. Orthopedic Trauma Treatment
    • Joint dislocation reduction
    • Acute fracture management
    • Wound debridement
    • ORIF / CRIF (internal fixation with pins or implants)
    • Non-surgical fracture care
  7. Orthopedic Stem Cell Therapy
    A regenerative approach to repair damaged bone, cartilage, ligaments, and tendons caused by injury or degenerative conditions.
  8. Tissue Engineering in Orthopedics
    The use of PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma), secretomes, or growth factor injections to accelerate healing and slow degenerative joint disease progression.
Advanced Surgical Technologies and Expertise

Medistra Hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art medical technologies that ensure precision and safety in orthopedic procedures.

They utilize Computer-Assisted Orthopedic Surgery (CAOS) to help surgeons accurately position instruments during operations.

Their Robotic-Assisted VELYS Solution for knee replacement surgery allows the use of a Closed System Implant, designed to last for decades with robotic precision.

Additionally, the hospital employs C-ARM technology to enhance intraoperative accuracy, efficiency, and safety during bone surgeries. These innovations support minimally invasive techniques and faster recovery times for patients.
