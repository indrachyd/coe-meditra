Center of Excellence

Cardiology

As a comprehensive healthcare center specializing in adult cardiovascular care, the Cardiology Center of Medistra Hospital offers a full range of services to treat various cardiac conditions, from diagnostic examinations, interventional procedures & surgery and cardiac rehabilitation.
The facility is supported by a team of experienced cardiologists, state-of-the-art medical technology and a strong commitment to patient safety and comfort.

Diagnostic & Screening Services

Our cardiology department offers a complete range of diagnostic tools to detect and monitor heart conditions:

  • Electrocardiography (ECG)
  • Holter monitoring (1, 3, and 7 days)
  • 24-hour Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitoring
  • Ankle Brachial Index
  • Treadmill Stress Test
  • Transthoracic Echocardiography (TTE)
  • Transesophageal Echocardiography (TEE)
  • Stress Echocardiography
  • Dobutamine Stress Echocardiography
  • Bubble Test
  • Cardiac MRI
  • Cardiac CT Scan & Coronary Angiography
  • Cardiac Genetic Testing (for diagnosis & screening)
Diagnosis & Treatment

Medistra Hospital is equipped with the latest medical technologies to support accurate diagnosis and effective treatment.

Treatment Room

Our procedure rooms are fitted with modern equipment to support doctors in performing necessary interventions to aid patient recovery.

Stress Test

A stress test is used to detect heart problems that may occur only during physical activity. During the test, patients walk on a treadmill at increasing speeds while their ECG, blood pressure, and heart rate are closely monitored.

Cardiac Medical & Surgical Services
    Coronary Heart Disease
    • Coronary Angiography
    • Percutaneous Coronary Intervention (stent placement) using Drug-Eluting Stents (DES) and novel bioresorbable stents (DynamX)
    • Advanced PCI using technologies such as Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS), Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT), Rotablator, Orbital Atherectomy System (OAS), and Intravascular Lithotripsy (IVL)

    Heart Rhythm Disorders (Arrhythmia)
    • Electrophysiology Studies
    • Radiofrequency Ablation
    • Implantable Cardiac Defibrillator (ICD)
    • Automatic Implantable Cardioverter-Defibrillator (AICD)

    Heart Surgery
    • Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG)
    • Conventional or minimally invasive valve surgery
    • Thoracic aortic aneurysm repair (TEVAR/EVAR)

    Heart Valve Disorders
    • TAVI (Transcatheter Aortic Valve Implantation)A non-surgical procedure to replace narrowed aortic valves using catheterization
    • TricValve. A non-surgical procedure for placing bicaval valves in the superior and inferior vena cava
    • Mitral Clip. A non-surgical procedure to repair mitral valve leakage using a special device via catheter
    • Tricuspid Clip. A non-surgical procedure to repair tricuspid valve leakage via catheterization

    LAA Closure

    A procedure to close the left atrial appendage, especially for atrial fibrillation patients at high risk of stroke.


    CRTD (Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillator)

    An implantable device for heart failure patients at high risk of sudden cardiac death. This device works as a biventricular pacemaker and provides shocks during rapid heartbeats or ventricular fibrillation.


    Cardiac Rehabilitation

    An implantable device for heart failure patients at high risk of sudden cardiac death. This device works as a biventricular pacemaker and provides shocks during rapid heartbeats or ventricular fibrillation.

24-Hour Cardiac Emergency Services

Medistra Hospital offers 24/7 Cardiac Emergency Services with international standards, including Primary PCI (Percutaneous Coronary Intervention) to treat heart attacks in under 90 minutes (door-to-balloon time).
